Salmon Tacos with Mango Salsa

Description

This tacos are an amazing and complete meal with a lot of flavor

This are not the usual meat tacos but fish tacos

Ingredients

  • 3 mangoes, diced
  • 2 peaches, diced
  • 3 poblano peppers, seeded and diced
  • ½ red onion, diced, or more to taste
  • ½ lime, juiced, or more to taste
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped, divided
